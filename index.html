482 SID Visa Salary Threshold Calculator (Australia)

The 482 Skills in Demand (SID) visa requires sponsored workers to be paid at least the relevant income threshold and the Annual Market Salary Rate (AMSR). Many applicants are unsure whether their offer meets the Core Skills Income Threshold (CSIT) or Specialist Skills Income Threshold (SSIT), especially after the 1 July 2025 indexation.

This free calculator instantly checks your offered salary against the official 482 SID visa thresholds and your market rate, helping you quickly assess whether your remuneration is likely to satisfy nomination requirements. Simply choose your stream, enter your salary, and compare it to the required minimum in seconds.

FAQ

Does superannuation count toward the threshold?
Threshold checks are typically against annual earnings/salary amounts as defined by Home Affairs instruments and policy. In practice, many employers compare against base salary (excluding super). If in doubt, confirm with your sponsor or migration professional. [oai_citation:7‡Immigration and citizenship Website](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/employing-and-sponsoring-someone/sponsoring-workers/nominating-a-position/salary-requirements?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
What if my offered salary meets CSIT/SSIT but is below AMSR?
You can still fail the requirement if the role is paid below the market rate for an equivalent Australian worker. That’s why this calculator uses the higher of threshold and AMSR. [oai_citation:8‡Immigration and citizenship Website](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/employing-and-sponsoring-someone/sponsoring-workers/nominating-a-position/salary-requirements?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
Can this calculator determine my occupation eligibility?
No — it only checks salary math. Occupation eligibility is determined by the relevant skilled occupation lists/instruments and your circumstances.
